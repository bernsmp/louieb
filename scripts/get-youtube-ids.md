# How to Get YouTube Video IDs

## Option 1: From Playlist URL (Easiest)

1. Go to your YouTube playlist
2. Copy the playlist URL (looks like: `https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLxxxxx`)
3. The playlist ID is the part after `list=`

**Example:**
- URL: `https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL7HfhnqHyzRmGDUMDhcSgZW8pR7DhW_Hl`
- Playlist ID: `PL7HfhnqHyzRmGDUMDhcSgZW8pR7DhW_Hl`

## Option 2: Get Individual Video IDs

### Method A: From Video URL
1. Click on a video in your playlist
2. Copy the URL (looks like: `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xxxxx&list=PLxxxxx`)
3. The video ID is the part after `v=`

**Example:**
- URL: `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ&list=PL7HfhnqHyzRmGDUMDhcSgZW8pR7DhW_Hl`
- Video ID: `dQw4w9WgXcQ`

### Method B: From Playlist Page
